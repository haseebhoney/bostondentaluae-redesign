About Boston Dental Center

A Boutique Dental Experience Unlike Any Other

Strategically located in the heart of Abu Dhabi city, yet nestled in a quiet setting that provides both convenience and privacy, Boston Dental Center is your to-go dentistry boutique offering a range of comprehensive services in a friendly and relaxed atmosphere.

  • 9 expert specialists across all dental disciplines
  • CEREC® CAD/CAM technology for same-day restorations
  • Accepting MetLife, GeoBlue, HTH, Henner & more
  • Tabby & Tamara flexible payment plans available
  • Arabic-speaking staff — Arabic website available
  • Open 6 days a week with extended hours until 9PM
