Our Dental Services

At Boston Dental Center, we pride ourselves on keeping up-to-date with the latest knowledge and techniques to diagnose and treat a wide range of conditions. We are fully equipped to help our patients restore their oral health and their beautiful smiles.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Care Under One Roof

From routine checkups to complete smile transformations — our team of 9 specialists covers every aspect of modern dentistry, using the latest technology and techniques.

🦷
Dental Implants

Dental implants are the best treatment known to replace a lost tooth. Titanium fixtures surgically placed in the jawbone serve as artificial roots, supporting natural-looking crowns, bridges or full dentures. Permanent, comfortable and life-changing.

Learn More →
😁
Orthodontic Treatment

Orthodontics is associated with the diagnosis and treatment of deformities related to the teeth, face and jaws. We offer traditional braces and modern clear aligner options for children, teenagers and adults seeking a straighter, more confident smile.

Learn More →
🔬
Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment removes infected or inflamed pulp from inside the tooth. The pulp chamber and root canals are then thoroughly cleaned, disinfected and sealed — saving the natural tooth and eliminating pain with modern, virtually pain-free techniques.

Learn More →
🌿
Gingival (Gum) Treatment

Healthy gums are the foundation of a healthy smile. Our periodontists provide expert diagnosis and management of gum disease at all stages — from early gingivitis to advanced periodontitis — using the most effective, minimally invasive techniques.

Learn More →
✨
Teeth Whitening & Cleaning

Professional teeth whitening is a cosmetic dental procedure designed to lighten and brighten teeth that have been stained or discoloured. Our professional-grade treatments are safe, effective and can brighten your smile by several shades in a single visit.

Learn More →
💎
Cosmetic Restoration

Boston Dental Center is proud to have the artistry and expertise to create stunning smile transformations. Using veneers, composite bonding, and tailored smile design, we craft restorations that complement your facial features and exceed your expectations.

Learn More →
⚡
Ceramic Reconstruction by Computer (CEREC®)

Using state-of-the-art CAD/CAM technology, we design, mill and place ceramic dental restorations in a single appointment. No messy impressions, no temporary crowns, no second visit. CEREC® allows us to restore your tooth with a perfectly fitted, natural-looking ceramic crown or inlay in just a few hours.

Learn More →
🦴
Temporomandibular Disorder (TMD) Treatment

TMD affects the jaw joint and surrounding muscles, causing pain, clicking, restricted movement and headaches. Our specialists provide comprehensive diagnosis and a range of effective treatments from occlusal splints to advanced therapeutic interventions.

Learn More →
👶
Children's Dental Treatment

Creating positive dental experiences for children is one of our greatest priorities. Our gentle, friendly approach helps young patients feel safe and at ease — building the foundation for a lifetime of excellent oral health through education, prevention and gentle care.

Learn More →
🫦
Simple & Surgical Extraction

A tooth extraction is performed when a tooth is too damaged or decayed to be saved. Our experienced surgeons perform both simple and complex surgical extractions — including impacted wisdom teeth — with maximum precision and minimum discomfort.

Learn More →
